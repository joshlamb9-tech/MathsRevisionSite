Additional is a single calculator paper (60 minutes) for those who want to push the limits of their mathematical ability. It covers index laws, expanding and factorising, and Pythagoras' theorem. You'll need solid Core knowledge before tackling these topics.

  • Inequalities

  • Similar Shapes
  • Vectors
  • Circle Theorems
  • Functions and Graph Transformations
