Core

Core has two papers: Paper 1 is non-calculator (60 minutes, 40 marks) and Paper 2 is calculator-allowed (60 minutes, 60 marks). Topics include ratio, percentages, algebra, geometry, probability, sequences, and more. Core includes everything in Foundation plus the topics below.
