Foundation Formula Sheet

Press Ctrl+P (or Cmd+P on Mac) to print this sheet. Wait for equations to appear before printing.

Averages

Mean: $\text{Mean} = \dfrac{\text{Sum of values}}{\text{Number of values}}$

Range: $\text{Range} = \text{Largest value} - \text{Smallest value}$

Fractions

Adding/subtracting: Find a common denominator first. $\dfrac{a}{c} + \dfrac{b}{c} = \dfrac{a+b}{c}$

Multiplying: $\dfrac{a}{b} \times \dfrac{c}{d} = \dfrac{ac}{bd}$

Dividing (KCF — Keep, Change, Flip): $\dfrac{a}{b} \div \dfrac{c}{d} = \dfrac{a}{b} \times \dfrac{d}{c} = \dfrac{ad}{bc}$

Prime Factors

Every integer can be written as a product of prime factors. Use a factor tree to find them.

Example: $24 = 2^3 \times 3$

Rounding & Estimation

Rounding: Look at the digit to the right of the place you are rounding to. ≥ 5: round up. < 5: round down.

Estimation: Round each number to a convenient round number, then calculate.

Example: $318 \times 47 \approx 300 \times 50 = 15\,000$

Long Multiplication

Partition the second number, multiply each part, then add.

Example: $347 \times 23 = 347 \times 20 + 347 \times 3 = 6940 + 1041 = 7981$
